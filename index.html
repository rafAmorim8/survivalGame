Survival Game Instructions

Game Objective:

Try to survive as long as you can by not letting the monsters hit you. Use the keys WASD to move around the field.
You can also shoot the mosters by aiming at them with the mouse cursor and left-clicking the mouse.


Game Mechanics:

The Hero starts with 30 HP (health) and looses a portion of that every time that a monster hits him.
From time to time a healt booster pack appears and can provide a 10 Hp boost if you can get to it before it desapears.

You earn points by killing the monsters and by the amount of time you have survived.

The monsters will start to appear more frequently and will get faster as the time passes.

Game Controls:

W: Move up

A: Move Left

S: Move Down

D: Move Right

R: Reload (it takes 2.5 seconds to reload the gun)

Left-click: Shoot Gun
